FAQ

Common Questions

Our rates start from as low as 0.4% + 5p per transaction for debit cards. The exact rate depends on your business type, transaction volume, and average transaction value. We'll provide a personalised quote based on your specific needs.
Most of our providers offer next-day settlement, meaning funds reach your bank account within 24 hours of the transaction. Some even offer same-day settlement for an additional fee.
Contract terms vary by provider. We offer both short-term rolling contracts (30 days) and longer-term agreements with better rates. We'll explain all options so you can choose what works best for your business.
You can accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and UnionPay. Contactless payments including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay are also supported.
Most applications are approved within 24-48 hours. Once approved, your card terminal can be delivered within 2-3 working days. Online payment gateways can often be set up the same day.
